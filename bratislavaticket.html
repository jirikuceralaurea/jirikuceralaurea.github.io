How to buy tickets in Bratislava?

Prices

Ticket validity Price for paper Price for electronic
30 minutes 0.90€ 0.81€
60 minutes 1.30€ 1.18€
24 hours 4€ 3.70€
72 hours 9€ 8.20€
These are the most useful types of tickets but there are more - there is an integrated transport system with nearby towns, so there are different zones for which you can also buy tickets. These prices, however, give you an idea about the prices of traveling within the city itself.

Machines

There are machines for ticket purchases in different places - on many bus or tram stops, at the train station, bus station or airport. Some of them (the newer ones) accept cards but most don't, so do not rely on that.

"Tapping" in vehicles

In all vehicles, you can currently use the option to "tap" with a payment card. You "tap" on the machine and you are charged 1€ for each tap. You will be charged at most 4€/day for tapping.

Mobile app

You can purchase all types of tickets also in the IDS BK app - from short-term ones up to yearly passes.
